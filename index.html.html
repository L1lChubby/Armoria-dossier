The Armorian Dominion

1.1Who We Are

Armorian is more than a military force.

It's more than a fleet, a corporation, or a single world.

Armorian is a civilization.

Spread across multiple star systems, our people live on colonies, orbital stations, industrial worlds, research facilities, and deep-space habitats. While countless factions fight over territory, resources, and power, Armorian exists to create something that lasts.

Our goal has never been conquest.

Our goal is stability.

The galaxy is unpredictable. Borders change. Governments rise and fall. Entire systems can disappear into chaos overnight.

Armorian was built to stand against that chaos.

We protect our people, advance our technology, and secure the future of humanity among the stars.

1.2How Armorian Began

Nobody knows exactly when the first Armorian settlement was founded.

Records from that era are fragmented, and much of humanity's early expansion into space has been lost to time.

What is known is that centuries ago, many colonies became isolated from one another. Trade collapsed, communication failed, and conflicts broke out across countless systems.

Some worlds survived.

Many didn't.

As these fractured colonies struggled to endure, several systems united under a shared belief:

Humanity's future depended on cooperation rather than division.

Over time, those systems grew into a larger alliance.

That alliance eventually became the Armorian Dominion.

Since then, Armorian has expanded steadily throughout known space, establishing new colonies, protecting trade routes, and building the infrastructure needed to support life across the stars.

1.3What We Believe

Armorian is built upon four principles.

Unity

Humanity accomplishes more together than apart.

Stability

Prosperity cannot exist without order.

Progress

The future belongs to those willing to build it.

Duty

Every citizen has a role in protecting what we have created.

These principles guide everything from military operations to scientific research and colonial development.

1.4Aether Energy

If there is one thing that made Armorian possible, it is Aether.

Aether is an extremely powerful energy source discovered during the early years of Armorian expansion. It powers our fleets, our colonies, our orbital infrastructure, and the vast transportation networks connecting our territory.

Without Aether, modern Armorian civilization simply wouldn't exist.

Unfortunately, Aether comes with a cost.

The refining process is dangerous.

Even with advanced containment systems, a major failure can destroy entire facilities in seconds.

Because of this risk, Armorian's energy infrastructure is kept far away from populated worlds.

Most Aether is refined aboard large orbital stations built specifically for the task.

These stations keep our civilization running.

They're also some of the most heavily targeted locations in Armorian space.

The Armorian Defense Corps

The Armorian Defense Corps, often shortened to the ADC, serves as the military arm of the Dominion.

Their responsibilities range from defending colonies and escorting convoys to protecting strategic installations and responding to hostile incursions.

While many outsiders view the ADC as simply another military, its purpose is much broader.

The Defense Corps exists to protect the people and infrastructure that allow Armorian civilization to survive.

Every colony defended.

Every station protected.

Every trade route secured.

All of it serves a single purpose:

Keeping humanity's future alive among the stars.

Enemies of Armorian

Not everyone agrees with Armorian's vision.

Some see the Dominion as a force for stability.

Others see it as an obstacle.

Over the years, numerous groups have challenged Armorian authority.

Pirate syndicates raid shipping lanes and energy convoys.

Separatist movements seek independence from Dominion control.

Radical insurgents target critical infrastructure in hopes of weakening Armorian influence.

Foreign powers compete for territory, resources, and strategic advantages throughout known space.

Because of these threats, conflict remains a constant reality on the frontier.

Operation: Meltdown

5.1Aether Refinement Platform Seven

Far beyond Armorian's major population centers, drifting above a frozen frontier world, sits Aether Refinement Platform Seven.

Most people simply call it Platform Seven.

At first glance, it appears to be just another industrial station.

In reality, it is one of the most important facilities in the entire sector.

Platform Seven processes and stores massive quantities of refined Aether energy before distributing it to nearby colonies, stations, and military installations.

Millions depend on its continued operation, whether they realize it or not.

Because of the danger involved in refining Aether, the station was deliberately constructed far from populated systems. If something went wrong here, the damage would be contained.

At least, that was the idea.

5.2Why It Is Constantly Attacked

Platform Seven contains something every enemy wants:

Energy.

Not credits.

Not weapons.

Energy.

The station's reserves are valuable enough to power fleets, sustain colonies, or cripple entire sectors if destroyed.

For pirates, it's an opportunity.

For insurgents, it's a target.

For rival powers, it's leverage.

As a result, Platform Seven has become one of the most contested facilities on the frontier.

5.3The Facility

The station is divided into three major sectors.

Communications Sector

The outermost section of the station.

This area houses long-range communications systems, sensor arrays, and monitoring equipment used to maintain contact with nearby fleets and installations.

If these systems fail, the station becomes isolated.

Reinforcements become difficult to coordinate.

The rest of the station becomes vulnerable.

Energy Transfer Sector

The industrial heart of Platform Seven.

This area handles the movement, storage, and processing of refined Aether cores.

Cargo systems move energy throughout the station while maintenance crews monitor containment systems around the clock.

Most day-to-day station operations happen here.

It is also where attackers gain access to the reactor network.

Reactor Sector

The most heavily defended area aboard the station.

Deep within the facility sits the primary reactor complex responsible for stabilizing and distributing Aether throughout the platform.

Everything depends on it.

If the reactor fails, the station dies with it.

5.4The Meltdown Incident

During the events now known as Operation Meltdown, hostile forces launched a coordinated assault against Platform Seven.

Their objective was simple:

Disable communications.

Breach containment systems.

Push the reactor into catastrophic overload.

What followed became one of the largest security failures in the station's history.

Whether the attackers succeed or whether the Defense Corps manages to hold the line depends entirely on the outcome of the battle.

5.5The Future

Platform Seven is only one facility among thousands spread throughout Armorian territory.

Shipyards, research stations, mining colonies, defense arrays, and frontier settlements all play a role in the survival of the Dominion.

Every location has a story.

Every facility has a purpose.

And every one of them may someday become a battlefield.
